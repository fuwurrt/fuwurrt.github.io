Popular songs included in the game

Over here, we will introduce you to some of the most popular songs of the game. Either they are originals, or they are collabs from other games.
There are also plenty of well-known BOF/BOFU songs too!

  1. Phigros originals

      2. Chapter 8 chronicles

    One of the most recent and well-made chapter to the sequence of the main story, Chapter 8 brings 5 brand new songs to the game, with the following 3 attracting many people:

    a. Distorted Fate - Sakuzyo
    Sakuzyo is one of the biggest rhythm game musician, featured in varieties of games such as Arcaea or CHUNITHM, this song was a real pleasant surprise to Phigros players.

    b. Retribution - Kry.exe
    The song has been selected as a "prequel" to the 2024 April Fools of the game.

    c. DESTRUCTION 3,2,1 - Normal1zer vs. Broken Nerdz
    With the genre being speedcore, the song currently holds the highest base BPM of 321.321.

      Chapters "boss" songs

    They are given these names, as they are reqiured to unlock and cleared before moving to another chapter in the main story chronicles.

    a. Spasmodic - 姜米條☆颶風~♫元力上人♫
    The word "spasmodic" is used to describe something that occurs or is done in brief, irregular bursts. (weirdly explained the charts but okay just watch it and you will understand).

    b. Igallta - Se-U-Ra
    According to the developers, this song's chart is "a collaborative effort of almost all charters." In addition, this song can also be found in WAVEAT ReLIGHT, Muse Dash, KALPA, Lanota, OverRapid, Rotaeno and TAKUMI³ as part of their respective collaborations with Phigros.

    c. Rrhar'il - Team Grimoire
    Just so you know, Team Grimoire is only one person. He has made plenty of original works for other games like maimai or Arcaea.
    This song was placed 1st in "Your Favorite Phigros Song Awards 2022" poll conducted in China.

  2. Collaborations

    3. Phigros has several collab "chapters" with several other rhythm games and/or artists. Below will be 3 of the most popular collabs (in my opinion) of the game.

      BassAreUs

    A collaboration with かめりあ (Camellia), one of the biggest composers every in the rhythm gaming community, this collab has brought surprises to players. One of the most played songs is +ERABY+E CONNEC+10N, included in the Tera I/O album of Camellia.

      CHUNITHM collection

    SEGA's famous rhythm arcade game, despite only having 3 songs included, the picks were rather interesting.
    One of them is TECHNOPOLIS 2085, made by PRASTIK DANCEFLOOR. It has raised quite interesting arguments of the songs being "wobbly", yet it still is unique.

      Lanota collection

    Sometimes known as the "dishwashing" game, as the area is a circle spinning around and all (haha maimai reference).
    With the introduction of 5 songs, You are the Miserable by t+pazolite grabs the majority's attention, as the composer is well-known in several games.

  3. April Fools

    4. Phigros is sometimes also known for the gimmicks that they have done for this unannouced "holiday". Here are a look through 3 years of post-Legacy (version 3.0.0 onwards) April Fools.

      2022 April Fools

    There are two songs included, the first one - Sigma (Haocore Mix) ~ 105秒の伝説 - is the gateway song, where you need to achieve a score between 600.000-699.999 to unlock the main focal point of this year: Sigma (Haocore Mix) ~ Regrets of the Yellow Tulip ~. Consisted of 4000 notes, this was the one that included the highest note count ever in the game. In addition, the chart did not have the presence of the judgement line.

      2023 April Fools

    The gateway song this time being 雪降り、メリクリ, just a normal song in the Single collection of the game. Yet, the gimmick starts when the difficulty is shown as "26°C" on the bottom right. When clicked, an AC remote will appear. Here, there are two things you can do:
    First, if you try to increase the temperature, the screen will become red and wobbly(mimicking a hot weather environment), and when 40°C is reached, you will be brought back to the selection screen.
    But if you decrease it, at 0°C, it will change to the gimmick song of this year: 今年も「雪降り、メリクリ」目指して頑張ります！！
    At first, it appears to be a LV. 12 song, pretty simple for the majority, but when you reach 240 notes, it changes to a ? (it means good luck with that)

      2024 April Fools

    Pretty simple to access this year's song, with the gateway song being Retribution. Press on Mirror mode repeatedly on this song until the background turns pink. Then when you click on play, the remix - Retribution ~ Cycle of Redemption ~ - will appear.
